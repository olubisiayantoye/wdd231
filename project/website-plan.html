Discover Nigeria - Website Plan

1. Site Name

Discover Nigeria - This name reflects the mission to showcase Nigeria’s culture, landscapes, and history.

2. Site Purpose

The website provides a digital hub for exploring Nigeria’s tourist attractions, traditions, cuisine, history, travel tips, and events.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the must-visit tourist attractions in Nigeria?
  • Where can I find traditional Nigerian dishes and recipes?

4. Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#006400) - Used for headings and accents.
  • Beige (#F5F5DC) - Used for background highlights.

5. Typography

  • Heading Font: Montserrat (for titles and section headings)
  • Body Font: Open Sans (for paragraphs and descriptions)
